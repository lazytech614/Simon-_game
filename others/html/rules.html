Rules to play "Simon Game"

  • ==> Simon is a memory game where you need to repeat a sequence of colors and sounds.

  • ==> The game starts with one random color and sound being shown by Simon.

  • ==> Your job is to repeat the sequence by clicking or tapping on the colors in the same order.

  • ==> If you get the sequence right, Simon adds another random color and sound to the sequence, making it longer.

  • ==> You continue repeating the sequence as it gets longer and longer.

  • ==> If you make a mistake and click the wrong color or get the sequence wrong, you lose the round.

  • ==> The game keeps track of how many rounds you've completed successfully.

  • ==> Your goal is to see how many rounds you can successfully complete without making a mistake.

  • ==> Have fun and challenge yourself to beat your high score!
